    ❓ Help Guide / ସାହାଯ୍ୟ ଗାଇଡ
    How every button works on this page
    Header Buttons
    ☀️
    Theme Toggle (Dark / Light)
    Switch between dark mode (black background) and light mode (white background). Your preference is saved automatically for next visit.
    ❓
    Help Button
    Opens this help dialog explaining all features. Available in English and Odia.
    🌐
    EN / ଓଡ଼ Language Toggle
    Switch the interface between English and Odia. Disease names, remedies and all labels will change to your chosen language.
    📋
    History Button
    View all your past plant analyses — shows disease detected, confidence, and remedy for each scan. Only visible when you are logged in.
    🟢
    User Chip (your name + ×)
    Shows your logged-in username with a green dot. Click × to log out securely.
    Upload & Analysis
    🖼️
    Drop Zone (Upload Area)
    Click to browse files, drag & drop an image onto this area, or paste an image from clipboard (Ctrl+V). Accepted formats: PNG, JPG, WEBP, BMP. Max size: 16MB.
    🔬
    Analyze Plant Button
    Sends your leaf image to the AI model for disease detection. The button is disabled until you upload an image. A spinner shows while processing.
    Results Panel
    ✅
    Status Bar (Healthy / Disease)
    Green ✅ means your plant is healthy. Orange ⚠️ means a disease was detected. Shown at the top of results.
    📊
    Confidence Bar
    Shows how certain the AI is about its prediction (0–100%). Higher % means more reliable result. The bar fills visually to represent the score.
    🏆
    Top 3 Predictions
    The AI's top 3 guesses ranked by confidence. Helps you see if there is ambiguity between similar diseases.
    💊
    Recommended Remedy
    Practical treatment advice for the detected disease — including fungicides, pesticides, or cultural practices. Shown in your selected language.
    ↩️
    Analyze Another Button
    Clears the current result and resets the upload area so you can scan a new leaf image.
